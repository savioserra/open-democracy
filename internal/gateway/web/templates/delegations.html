Delegations

Liquid democracy: delegate your vote to someone you trust for a specific scope. You can have a different delegate at each scope level — like having different representatives at neighborhood, city, state, and federal levels. Your delegate votes on your behalf, but if you vote directly on a bill, your direct vote always wins. Delegations are depth-1 (no transitive chains — your delegate must vote personally) and revocable at any time.

Grounded in the Brazilian Constitution, Art. 1, sole paragraph: "All power emanates from the people, who exercise it through elected representatives or directly."
